Abanyeshuri ba Jill Barham Barigaragaza mu Gikorwa cy’Impano cyo Gutangiza Umwaka

Gutangira umwaka mushya neza cyane, Jill Barham School yateguye ibirori by’impano bishimishije, bigaragaza impano zitandukanye z’abanyeshuri bayo. Ibirori byarimo ibikorwa byinshi bitandukanye birimo kuririmba, kubyina, amayeri y'ubumaji, no gukina ibyuma bya muzika. Abanyeshuri bafashe urubyiniro bafite ishyaka, bashimisha abarebye imbyino zabo n'ubumenyi. Ibi birori ntibyabaye urubuga gusa rw’abanyeshuri rwo kwigaragaza, ahubwo byateye kandi umuco w’ubumwe n’ishyaka ry’ishuri. Ababyeyi, abarimu, ndetse n'abanyeshuri bagenzi babo bashimishijwe n’ibyo bari barebye, bituma bigirwa ibirori byibukwa n’impano hamwe n’ubufatanye. Ibi birori byashyize umwuka mwiza mu ntangiriro z’umwaka, bitera abanyeshuri gushishikarizwa gukomeza kwiga no gushyigikira impano zabo z'umwihariko.

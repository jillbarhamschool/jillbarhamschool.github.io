TOR

Tourism.

Kwigira ibijyanye n’ubukerarugendo birimo kwiga ku mico y’isi yose, imyitwarire y’ingendo irambye, n’urwego rw’amahoteli. Bitanga ubumenyi ku buryo ubukerarugendo bugira ingaruka ku bukungu no ku bidukikije. Abanyeshuri bakora imyitozo mu byerekeranye no kwita ku bakiriya, kwamamaza, no gutegura ibirori, bibategurira imirimo myiza mu gice kizamuka. Iyi nyigisho itanga amahirwe yo kwiga imico itandukanye no kugira uruhare mu bikorwa by’ubukerarugendo burambye.

NIT

Netwoking And Internet Technology.

Kwiga ibijyanye n’imiyoboro n’ikoranabuhanga rya Internet muri Jill Barham School biguha ubumenyi bw’ingenzi ku isi y’ikoranabuhanga y’iki gihe. Uzamenya byinshi ku bijyanye no gushushanya imiyoboro, umutekano wa cyber, no gukoresha ibicu by'ikoranabuhanga, byose bigushoboza kuyobora no kurinda imiyoboro ya mudasobwa. Iyi nyigisho itanga amahirwe menshi y’akazi ashyirwa imbere mu rwego rwa IT support, ubuyobozi bw’imiyoboro, no gusesengura uburyo bwa mudasobwa. Ni amahitamo meza ku muntu wese ushaka kubaka ahazaza he mu ikoranabuhanga n’udushya!

FBO

Food And Biverage Operations.

Kwiga Ibijyanye n’Imikorere y’Ibiribwa n’Ibinyobwa birimo kwiga ku micungire no gutanga serivisi z’ibiribwa n’ibinyobwa mu bihe bitandukanye nka resitora, amahoteri, n’ibirori. Uyu mwuga urimo ibijyanye no gutegura urutonde rw’amafunguro, umutekano w'ibiribwa, kugenzura ibiciro, no kwita ku bakiriya. Abanyeshuri bakora imyitozo mu kuyobora ibikorwa byo mu gikoni no mu isomero, bibafasha kugenzura ubuziranenge n’ubushobozi. Iyi nyigisho ibategurira imirimo mu rwego rw’amahoteri, harimo imyanya mu micungire, gutegura ibirori, no mu buhanga bwo guteka.

ACC

Accounting.

Kwiga ibijyanye n’ibaruramari birimo kwiga ku birebana no gutanga raporo z’imari, ubugenzuzi, amategeko y’imisoro, no gucunga ingengo y’imari. Bituma abanyeshuri babona ubumenyi bwo gusuzuma amakuru y’imari, gutegura inyandiko z’imari, no kwemeza ko bubahiriza amategeko n’amabwiriza. Uyu mwuga ni ingenzi mu micungire y’imari mu bucuruzi, leta, no mu miryango idaharanira inyungu. Abize barangiza amashuri biteguye akazi mu baruramari, ubugenzuzi, isesengura ry’imari, no kugira inama, bakagira uruhare mu buzima bwiza bw’imari n’intsinzi y’imiryango yabo.

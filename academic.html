Jill Barham School Students Explore Nyungwe Nziza Ecolodge.

Recently, students from Jill Barham School embarked on an educational trip to the Nyungwe Nziza Ecolodge in Kitabi, Rwanda. The trip aimed to provide students with hands-on learning experiences about environmental conservation and sustainable living. During their stay, students participated in various activities, including guided nature walks through the lush Nyungwe Forest, engaging workshops on eco-friendly practices, and interactive sessions with local conservationists. The students also enjoyed cultural exchanges with the lodge staff, gaining insights into the local community's efforts to preserve their natural heritage. This enriching experience left a lasting impression on the students, fostering a deeper appreciation for nature and the importance of environmental stewardship.
